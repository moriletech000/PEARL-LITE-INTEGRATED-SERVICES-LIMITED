Who We Are

Rooted in Nigeria.
Reaching the World.

Pearl-Lite Integrated Services Limited is a full-spectrum agro-industrial company based in Epe, Lagos State. We bridge the gap between Nigerian farmlands and global markets — supplying equipment, processing raw produce, and exporting premium agro-commodities that meet international compliance standards.

From our base at 1 Pearl-Lite Industries Avenue, Off No. 12 Ibowon Road, Iragushi, Eredo LGA, Epe, we serve farmers, processors, exporters, and buyers across Nigeria and beyond.
